Sex Offense Allegations

Few accusations are more serious than this one. What's at stake is not just jail or prison time. It's your name, your family, your career, and depending on the charge, a lifetime on a public registry that follows you to every home, every job, and every relationship you'll ever have. For some offenses, a conviction can mean decades in prison. For many, registration lasts for life, long after any sentence ends.

And unlike other charges, the damage starts before any conviction. An accusation alone can cost you your job, your reputation, and time with your own children. That's why these cases cannot wait, and cannot be handled quietly on hope.

These cases are defended with detail, preparation, and care. We take the State's case apart piece by piece, and we take the time to understand what actually happened and who is involved. Nothing gets accepted at face value, because in these cases, the difference between an accusation and the truth is everything.

If you've been contacted, investigated, or charged, call now. Everything you tell us is confidential. The consultation is free.

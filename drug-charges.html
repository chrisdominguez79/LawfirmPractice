Drug Charges

Charged with drug possession? Here's what's at stake: your record, your job, and depending on the charge, your freedom. But an arrest is just the State's opening move, and in drug cases, how the police came to find the evidence matters just as much as what they claim they found.

For a possession charge to hold up in court, the police had to get a lot of things right: the reason for the stop, the length of the stop, the reason for the search, where the evidence was found, how it was found, and whether the substance is even what the officers claim it is. We question everything. We review all the evidence, we demand the lab results, we check the chain of custody from the roadside to the courtroom, and when any of it falls short, we ask the court to act. One broken link, and the whole case can come apart.

Every drug case comes down to one of two fights: proving the State can't make its case, or showing them why they shouldn't. Whether that means attacking the evidence, telling your side of the story, or fighting for an outcome that keeps your record clean, you need someone who knows which fight to pick and how to win it. One phone call tells you where you stand. The consultation is free.

Occupational Driver's Licenses

If your license has been suspended, life doesn't stop. You still have to get to work, pick up your kids, and make it to the store. An occupational driver's license lets you legally drive for work, school, and essential household duties while your suspension is in effect.

Whatever caused the suspension, we handle the process from start to finish. Don't risk driving while suspended, because getting caught makes everything worse. One phone call gets it moving. The consultation is free.
